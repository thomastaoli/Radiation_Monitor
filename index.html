We're afraid of radiation... But why?

Welcome to Thomas's Radiation Monitor, a dedicated platform offering real-time insights into radiation levels across key Asian cities. The data is collected from multiple environment agencies, empower communities with accurate and accessible radiation data, fostering awareness and promoting informed decision-making for health and safety.

About the Project

Radiation is a natural part of our environment, yet elevated levels can pose significant health risks. In an era where environmental changes and nuclear activities are more prevalent, understanding radiation levels is crucial for safety and preparedness.
Data of this project is collected from trustworthy government agencies, which serves as a reliable source to guide your actions - whether you're a concerned citizen, a researcher, or a policy-maker.
